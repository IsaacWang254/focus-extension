## Design Context

### Users
This product is primarily for a single user: the project owner. It is used throughout the day while working on a computer to reduce distraction, keep attention on the right tasks, and make intentional choices around browsing, planning, and focus sessions.

### Brand Personality
Simple, calm, polished. The interface should feel clear and quietly premium, borrowing from the restraint of Google Chrome, ChatGPT, and Apple product surfaces. It should help with focus without feeling gamified, loud, or visually busy.

### Aesthetic Direction
Minimal, neutral, and intentional. Favor soft off-white and graphite surfaces, subtle borders, generous spacing, and highly legible typography. Use restrained motion and avoid decorative gradients, bright accent colors, or dense card-heavy layouts. The product should feel modern and useful first, with polish showing up through spacing, hierarchy, and materials rather than ornament.

### Design Principles
1. Reduce noise before adding emphasis.
2. Make hierarchy obvious through type, spacing, and contrast.
3. Keep controls familiar, calm, and fast to scan.
4. Use neutral surfaces with one restrained accent language.
5. Design for sustained daily use, not one-time visual impact.
